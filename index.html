Search & AI Visibility

Owning the Search Landscape — Today and Tomorrow

SEO isn't optional for a multi-location brand. And LLM visibility is the next competitive moat.

Local SEO

Google Business Profile Optimization Every location fully optimized: categories, attributes, services, photos, Q&A, posts. Consistency across all 5+ locations. This is the foundation of local visibility.
Review Velocity Program Systematic review generation: post-appointment SMS/email triggers, staff training on ask timing, response templates for all reviews. Target: 15+ new Google reviews per location per month.
Location Pages & Technical SEO Dedicated landing pages for each location with unique content, schema markup, internal linking. Technical audit: Core Web Vitals, crawl errors, indexation issues, site speed optimization.
Local Service Ads (LSAs) Google Guaranteed placement for every eligible location. Pay-per-lead model means zero wasted spend on clicks that don't convert. Direct phone calls and booking requests only.
Conquest & Competitor Targeting Bid on competitor brand terms. Target audiences who've visited competitor locations. Retarget people who searched for competitors but didn't book. Ethical, effective, standard practice.

LLM / AI Visibility

Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) Structure all website content to be extractable by LLMs. FAQ schema, how-to markup, concise answer formatting. When someone asks ChatGPT "best facial bar in NYC," Silver Mirror needs to be the answer.
AI Overview Optimization Google's AI Overviews are replacing traditional search results for many queries. Optimize content structure, authority signals, and citation patterns to appear in these AI-generated summaries.
Schema Markup Deployment LocalBusiness schema, Service schema, Review schema, FAQ schema on every location page. This is how search engines and LLMs understand what Silver Mirror offers and where.
Content Authority Building Publish expert content that LLMs cite: skincare guides, ingredient explainers, treatment comparisons. Position Silver Mirror as the authoritative source, not just a business listing.
SOLv (Share of LLM Voice) Tracking Monitor how often Silver Mirror appears in LLM responses vs. competitors. This is the new "share of voice" metric — and it's measurable today.
