Operating Infrastructure

Four Systems Running Daily

This runs continuously. No performance drop goes unnoticed.

Attribution Layer

Spend connects to enrolled members through Boulevard + ad platforms. UTM structure standardized across all locations. Revenue mapped at the location level — not blended averages. Every dollar has a traceable path.

Budget Allocation Logic

Budget moves between locations based on performance. Weekly reallocation toward lowest cost per enrolled member. High performers get scaled. Underperformers get diagnosed — not reflexively cut.

Creative Testing System

Creative is tested against enrollment rate — not CTR. Winning creative is scaled. Losing creative is cut. The system learns continuously — each cycle produces better signal than the last.

Monitoring System

Daily monitoring across all 10 locations. Anomaly detection for spend spikes, conversion drops, creative fatigue. Issues are caught before they compound — not after the monthly report.
