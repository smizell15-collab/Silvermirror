How I'll Operate
One engine.
Six connected systems.

Each system feeds the next. Sales outcomes drive channel priorities. Market-level research drives budget decisions. The loop never stops.

The principle

"I optimize for volume, quality, and sales — with signed membership enrollments as the leading metric. Every location is its own business."

The acquisition engine
01
Channel Strategy
Google captures demand. Meta creates it. TikTok builds brand pipeline. LSAs activated where SoLV is below 75% to maintain map pack visibility.
02
Budget Allocation
Each location has its own revenue goals and budget. Weekly optimization for volume, quality, and sales. Warm audiences from Boulevard layer alongside existing campaigns.
03
Creative System
3–5 new concepts weekly, briefed against test hypotheses. UGC and video creators aligned to weekly angles. Kill or scale within 72 hours.
04
Conversion Layer
Landing pages, booking flow, offer structure. Two funnels: single-service bookings and direct membership enrollment. The job ends at the signed member.
05
Sales Feedback Loop
Weekly sync with intake teams. Lead quality scoring feeds back into creative and audience strategy. Bing and Yelp audited specifically — both in scope but historically low-intent from paid placements.
06
AI + Search Visibility
Schema markup across all 10 location pages. GBP completeness audit for LSA eligibility. Monthly LLM visibility tracking — where Silver Mirror appears in ChatGPT, Perplexity, and AI Overviews before competitors own that space.
